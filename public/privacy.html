Privacy Policy

This policy explains what data OmniPublish collects, how it is used, and the choices you have.

1. Data We Collect

We may collect account details, profile information, connected platform data, draft content, publishing activity, and technical logs needed to run the service.

2. How We Use Data

  • Authenticate users and maintain sessions.
  • Publish and schedule content on connected platforms.
  • Provide analytics, diagnostics, and security protections.
  • Improve product reliability and performance.

3. Sharing

We share data with third-party platforms only as needed to deliver the service, such as publishing content through connected APIs.

4. Cookies and Storage

OmniPublish uses cookies and browser storage for authentication, security, and user preferences.

5. Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for the service, legal obligations, or legitimate business purposes.

6. Your Choices

You can request access, export, deletion, or correction of your data through the product's account and GDPR tools.

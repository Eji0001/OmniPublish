Data Deletion Instructions

If you want OmniPublish to delete your data, you can do it from inside the app or contact support. This page explains the process for platform review and user requests.

How to request deletion

  1. Log in to your OmniPublish account.
  2. Open the account or GDPR settings page.
  3. Choose Request deletion.
  4. Confirm the request. Deletion is scheduled after a 30-day grace period.

What gets deleted

  • Your account profile
  • Connected platform credentials and tokens
  • Drafts, posts, media references, and activity logs associated with your account

How to cancel

You can cancel a pending deletion request during the grace period by opening the account settings and selecting Cancel deletion.

Need help?

If you are unable to access your account, contact us at support@omnipublish.io and we will process your request manually within 30 days.
